    Debt Service

    FY26 Proposed: $146,862

    Change from FY25: $0 (0%)

    Percentage of Total Budget

    1%

    Justifications for Major Changes

    The overall Debt Service budget for FY26 remains level-funded at $146,862 compared to FY25, though there are significant shifts within the debt structure:

    1. Short-Term Interest (Inside Levy): The 38% increase ($5,515) reflects current market interest rates, which have risen compared to previous fiscal years.
    2. Principal on Short-Term Debt: The 50% decrease (-$25,000) represents the continued pay-down of short-term borrowing obligations within the levy limit, demonstrating prudent debt management.
    3. School Roof Project: Principal payments increased by 111% ($27,862) due to the debt schedule established for this capital project, while interest payments decreased by 15% (-$8,377) as the principal is paid down. This debt is "outside the levy" as it was previously approved through a debt exclusion vote.
