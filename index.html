🔍 Where Your Money Goes

We are committed to **100% transparency** in how Hubbardston allocates taxpayer dollars. Click the sections below to see detailed spending.

Real estate taxes make up **89%** of our total revenue.

  • 📈 Tax Levy: $11,918,883
  • 🏛 Excluded Debt: $197,357
  • 🏛 State Aid: $400,820
  • 💵 Local Receipts: $750,000

Education accounts for **60%** of our budget, funding **Quabbin Regional School District & Monty Tech.**

Police, Fire, and EMS services receive **20%** of total expenditures, ensuring a safe and secure Hubbardston.

Investments in roads, municipal facilities, and equipment upgrades total **$1.3 million.**

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

The **Select Board, Finance Committee, and Town Administrator** work together to develop the budget based on projected revenue and community needs.

The tax levy increases by **2.5% annually** under Proposition 2½ to sustain essential services and capital projects.

Major planned projects include **Public Safety Building improvements, road repairs, and facility upgrades**.
